Pastry




Ingrediants:

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Milk
  • Sugar
    • Eggs

Recipie:

  1. Preheat the oil in a deep frying pan or a deep fryer to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, and yeast.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and melted butter.
  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the dough rest in a warm place for 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.
  5. Roll out and use.
